How It Works!

Step 1: Check Instagram Bio for Drop Date.

  • The best way to know when orders are open is looking at our Instagram.
  • A date will also be listed at the very top of the 'Home' Page.
  • You will also know orders are open if you are able to see packs listed on the 'Shop' page. If nothing is listed on 'Shop' page, orders are closed.
  • We open orders about every 2 weeks.

Step 2: Choose your flavors!

  • We change out flavors once a month - keeping some of the classics like Chocolate Chip^2 and C.R.E.A.M.
  • Find our flavors on the 'Cookies' page

Step 3: Choose between:

  • Pick Up: At one of 3 Revolution Ice Cream locations.
  • Local Delivery: We will hand deliver in the Brandon, Riverview, Tampa area.
  • Shipping: We priority ship your pack to your door step!
